# Rule 8: Bilingual & RTL
The app ships EN/AR. Use the i18n helpers (`useT`) and provide both locales for new copy. Lay out with logical properties (`ms-*`/`me-*`, `text-start`) and flip direction-aware icons so RTL renders correctly.
